How To Setup A WordPress Website

WordPress is an open-source website creation tool based on PHP and MySQL, used to create a website that will be stored on a host server. WordPress is installed effortlessly in most hosts, usually with one click. After that, go to your WordPress and sign in to your dashboard to choose and apply the style you prefer. Always install important plugins to improve your site’s functionality, for instance, Yoast SEO for search engine optimization and Contact Form 7 for the forms.

Develop the major pages of your site, such as the Home and Contact, and prepare the templates for the site’s navigation system. As a reminder, always make necessary settings changes to make all the features operate correctly, and lastly, revisit your site before public exposure.

How Much Does It Cost To Have Someone Build A WordPress Website?

The price of creating a WordPress site varies. It depends on the site’s difficulty, design, and the programmer’s skill. It ranges from $500 to $5,000 for freelancers, $1,000 to $10,000 for small agencies, and $10,000 to $75,000 for big agencies. A single module can cost anywhere between $2,500 to $7,500, while larger agencies, or those that provide extensive configurations and functionalities, may charge $10,000 to $50,000 and above. Other costs may also be involved. These include domain registration, hosting, and premium themes or plugins. They usually cost extra. In general, it depends on the necessities of your website and the price offered by the service provider.
