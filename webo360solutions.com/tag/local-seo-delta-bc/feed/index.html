What Is Local SEO?

It means the optimization service that you invest in assists a business in enhancing its ranks within local search. The idea is to increase the probability that a local customer who searches for information online will be able to find a local business that meets his needs. Some key elements of search engine optimization include:

  • Ensuring that the listings on Google My Business and other local directories are up to date and accurate with the business name, address, phone number, website, and services.
  • Review signals such as reviews, citations, and others that let the search engines know the business location.
  • Developing content and web pages that contain a large number of local keywords and names of cities.
  • Promoting comments, posts, and shares that are related to the customers and which contain location tags.
  • Including optimizing on-page factors and website maps for local search purposes.
  • The main drive is to increase visibility in the local index and attract the attention of potential local customers using the Internet.

Difference Between SEO And Digital Marketing

SEO and digital marketing are quite closely related, but they are not actually the same thing that is used for marketing on the Internet. Here is a basic explanation of the difference:

SEO aims to improve a website’s rank in unpaid search results, like Google’s. Some of the SEO efforts are as follows: keyword studies, on-page optimization, and link building, among others. The aim is to enhance the exposure of your posted web pages to the normal searches carrying relevant keywords.

Digital marketing is a broader term. It means promoting goods or services using digital technologies. In reality, SEO is part of digital marketing. Other major forms of digital marketing include:

  • PPC or Pay per click Ads
  • Article Marketing (blogging and video etc)
  • Social media marketing
  • Email marketing 
  • Mobile/app marketing
  • Affiliate marketing

SEO focuses on getting more organic traffic from search engines. Digital marketing, however, aims to attract visitors using various methods. It also seeks to convert them into buyers and keep them as customers.

SEO is an element of the digital marketing mix that aims at driving search visibility. Digital marketing is also associated with SEO and other methods for obtaining and retaining customers through the use of the Internet. Both are, in fact, often used simultaneously, as they complement each other for increasing website traffic and sales.
