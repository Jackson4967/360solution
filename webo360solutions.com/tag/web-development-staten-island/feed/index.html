How Web Development Companies In Staten Island Developed Websites

Web designers in Staten Island typically follow traditional steps for web design and design services. Clients often request responsive websites so that their sites can be effectively used on any device. Here we are discussing the steps that are followed by the Staten Island web design agencies to help their clients to achieve business goals.

Planning

The process often starts with the first meeting with the client. The development team introduces themselves and asks about the goals, audience, and needs. This phase involves:

  • Understand the client’s requirements in terms of his/her expectations and the goal of the business.
  • Determining what the project is to include in terms of features, which functionality it is expected to possess, and the time within which the project is expected to be accomplished.
  • Identify expenses that are likely to occur in the course of the implementation of the project and work out how much is likely to be spent in the process.

Research

Once the goals and requirements are clear, the team conducts research to inform the development process:

  • Gathering information from popular websites of similar nature to analyze the trends of usage and their patterns
  • Know who the clients are so as to meet the need of designing the website to suit their needs.
  • Choose the right tech stack and tools for the client’s needs and values.

Design

The design phase focuses on creating the visual and structural elements of the website. This phase includes:

  • Creating more elaborate sketches, such as wireframes or mockups, that define the basic framework and design of the website.
  • Creating proper color combination problems, typeface selection, and many graphic solutions, such as choosing images and backgrounds.
  • Make sure that users have a good flow when going through the site.

Development

With the design approved, the development team starts building the website:

  • Use client-side coding, like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, to implement these elements. They will create the website’s design.
  • Creating the server-side elements at the back end of the website, as well as objects and databases, as well as languages meant for servers.
  • Acquiring CMS, if needed, a content management system, that is, WordPress, Joomla, or Drupal, so that the client can readily manage the content.

Testing

Before launching the website, thorough testing is conducted to ensure everything functions correctly:

  • Make sure all links and every interactive item or form and feature are fully operational as they should be.
  • Make sure the website is easily accessible across various browsers as well as different devices.
  • Evaluating the simple navigation of the site so that it can be loaded fast.

Launch

After testing and resolving any bugs, the website is ready for launch. This phase consists of two steps:

  • Uploading the website to the live server and setting up the domain name.
  • Conducting last inspections to verify that everything works properly once the site is online.
