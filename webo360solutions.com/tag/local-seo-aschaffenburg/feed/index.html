What Are The Three Types Of Local SEO Aschaffenburg?

The main three types of SEO are mentioned below.

On-Page SEO

This can be defined as the process of helping specific pages of a website rank better in search engines. This involves working on the content, the titles of the content, the meta description of the content, the headings, and internal links with the aim of winning relevant keywords in the process.

Off-Page SEO

This is to enhance the website’s external influence on the website’s authority and credibility. It involves link building from other relevant and reputable websites, social networking, and managing one’s own social image online.

Technical SEO

This relates to the structural and functional working of a website to enable the search engines to crawl over the site and index it properly. This evaluates site speed, mobile friendly, XML sitemaps, structure, and whether they are using schema markup appropriately.

What Are The Local SEO Services?

Local SEO services are specific methods and approaches designed to boost a company’s exposure in local search results. Some of the local SEO services are as follows.

Google My Business Optimization

Optimizing Google My Business to increase the reach, Localization data, and details such as the address, phone number, and operational hours.

Local Keyword Research

Determining the right keywords for people in the target locale and finding products or service providers.

On-Page Optimization

Modifying Web content text, headers, and descriptions with locally relevant search words to increase keyword relevance.

Local Listings And Citations

It’s important to make sure that the business is correctly listed across multiple platforms and directories, which assists in increasing its reputation and local SERP visibility.

Customer Reviews Management

Asking customers for positive feedback and engaging with reviews to improve reputation and local SEO.

Localized Content Creation

Coming up with blog posts, articles, and other relevant content that may likely attract local traffic.

Backlink Building

Building backlinks from local websites and organizations to enhance the site’s credibility and search engine ranking.

Mobile Optimization

Keep the website simple since most clients will be accessing it from their mobile devices.
