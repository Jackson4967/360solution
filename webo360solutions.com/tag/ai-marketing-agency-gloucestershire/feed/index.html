What Is AI Marketing?

AI marketing refers to the application of artificial intelligence to improve marketing strategies and efforts. It means using technology to analyze client data, forecast their behavior, and provide designed commercials and content. By automating processes like email campaigns and social media postings, AI enables firms to contact the right people at the right time, increasing marketing effectiveness and efficiency. Overall, AI marketing enables businesses to better engage with their audience and increase revenue.

What Is PPC Advertising?

Pay-per-click (PPC) advertising lets businesses market their items online. They pay a fee each time someone clicks their ad. PPC is often used on Google Ads and social media. It lets businesses create targeted ads that appear in search results or on websites. Advertisers pick keywords for their business and set a budget. This lets them reach clients actively seeking their services. This strategy generates rapid traffic to a website and complements other marketing tactics.
