Steps in the Custom Web Development Process

A custom-designed website is developed through various steps, each crucial in ensuring that the final product is in line with the clients’ requirements. Here’s a brief outline of the process:

1. Discovery and Planning

This is the phase where you must understand the client’s goals, business and users. Designers and developers collaborate together with the client to map out the site’s goals the features it will offer, as well as material specifications.

2. Design and Prototyping

The design phase is the process of making prototypes and mockups to envision how the site will appear. This is the time when colors, layout and the user interface (UI) are decided upon making sure that the site is appealing visually and in line with the company’s brand.

3. Development and Testing

During the development phase developers from the front and back end create the layout to life. The website is then coded with functionality added and integrations are added. After the website is developed, it is subjected to strict tests to warrant that it’s bug-free and runs smoothly on every device.

4. Launch and Maintenance

After the site has passed all tests, it’s released and goes live. After the launch, developers favor continuous maintenance as well as updates and assistance to warrant that the website is running at its peak.
