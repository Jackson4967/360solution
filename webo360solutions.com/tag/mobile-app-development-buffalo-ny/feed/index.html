Design And Prototyping

The next process is where the vision becomes graphic. UX and UI designers use the abilities of human-centered design to create easily navigable, visually appealing wireframes and prototypes. Development

After realizing the concept of what the site should look like, the development phase begins here. Buffalo’s talented pool of developers splits into two camps: iOS and Android.
