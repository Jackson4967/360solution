Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What types of web development services do you offer?

Our company builds custom websites with e-commerce solutions as well as integrates APIs and provides search engine optimization and ongoing website support.

How long will it take to build my website?

Website delivery timelines start at 1-2 weeks for basic designs but complex implementations stretch anywhere from 3-7 weeks and beyond.

Do you provide custom website design or use templates?

Yes, we design custom websites from scratch for each brand, we can implement standard templates according to client preferences.

Will my website be mobile-friendly?

Definitely, Our company develops responsive websites and optimizes them specifically for mobile device usability.

Do you offer e-commerce website development services?

Our team develops security-focused accessible e-commerce solutions through Shopify, WooCommerce, and bespoke platform development.

Will I be able to update my website myself?

Yes, Through a user-friendly content management system, we will build your website so you can manage updates without difficulty.

What happens if I need changes after my website is live?

Our team provides post-launch services, which include supporting updates and website maintenance for required modifications.

Can you help with website redesigns?

Our team provides website redesign solutions, enabling you to update and enhance the operation of your platform.

Do you optimize websites for SEO?

Yes, our team optimizes websites through the integration of rapid load times combined with mobile compatibility and appropriate search engine metadata.

Do you offer ongoing website maintenance?

Yes we offer services including ongoing maintenance support, which protects your website from security threats while maintaining its updated state and stable performance.

Will you handle website hosting?

Yes, we offer website hosting services or assist in setting up reliable hosting tailored to your needs.

I want to have an email signup form on my website. Would you offer this service?

Yes, our team integrates email signup forms on your website that support your selected marketing platform

Does my site need to be updated to HTTPS instead of HTTP?

The implementation of HTTPS provides stronger security measures that enhance user trust and improve SEO performance.

What would be the pricing of a dynamic website?

Website development expenses for dynamic websites range from $1,500 to $15,000 based on how complicated they are and what features will be included.

Are static websites expensive or not?

No static website is not too expensive. Exclusively, static websites begin at around $400, while customers need to pay more for dynamic websites’ enhanced flexibility.

Is there any future for AI in web development?

Yes, there is a future for AI in web development. AI changes web developers‘ work. It enables custom designs, automates tasks, and improves design systems.
